DIY

DIY is a "do it yourself concept that enables one to add value to a once rendered old fashioned or useless clothe.This allows the owner to save more and spend less.We are taking this concept to make for teenagers and even ladies who love to try out new styles and cultures and also get into the milenial world and giving them a unique look according to their preference.This allows you to save more money and time by having it done for them and also it is a cheaper option to a spectacular outfit.

Why?

We are a group of friends who really enjoyed the fashion cultures and also got intrigued by the idea that we could be able to make a whole trendy outfit from our old wardrobe instead of having to throw it all away and having to shop which is time consuming and also involves alot of money spent.This provides a cheaper and more appealing option for fashion enthusiasts.We normally work from home through online purchases and orders done.

Find Us

Apart from doing all our work from home, you can find us at the K1 klubhouse flea market on Sundays where we showcase our outfits as one of the vendors which actually attracts very many customers.We do this for those who want to see and confirm our work and to also get to interact with our customers and also widen our market apart from those who know us online.
